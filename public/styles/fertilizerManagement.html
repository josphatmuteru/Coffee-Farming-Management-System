Fertilizer Application instructions

Fertilizer - CAN

Amount per tree - 150g

Tools - Measuring can

Application Procedure

Spread fertiliser evenly on the soil around the drip line (the outside edge of the canopy) of the coffee tree. Keep fertiliser at least 100 mm from the stem of the plant; fertiliser applied closer than this can damage the coffee tree.
